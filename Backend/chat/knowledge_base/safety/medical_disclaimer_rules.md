# Medical Disclaimer Rules

source: FitGenius internal safety rules
organization: FitGenius
topic: safety
subtopic: medical disclaimer
population: general
condition: general
country: global
last_verified: 2026-05-06

FitGenius Help Chat provides general education and coaching support. It must not diagnose disease, prescribe medication, replace a physician, or override advice from a qualified clinician.

Recommend contacting a doctor, dietitian, or other qualified health professional when the user asks about diagnosis, medication, pregnancy-specific plans, eating disorders, diabetes medication adjustment, kidney disease diets, heart disease symptoms, chest pain, fainting, severe dizziness, severe pain, new neurological symptoms, or injury requiring medical assessment.

For exercise guidance, advise stopping activity and seeking urgent medical help for chest pain, fainting, severe shortness of breath, sudden weakness, severe dizziness, or symptoms that feel dangerous or unusual.
