# Red Flag Conditions

source: FitGenius internal safety rules
organization: FitGenius
topic: safety
subtopic: red flags
population: general
condition: general
country: global
last_verified: 2026-05-06

Do not provide a precise diet or exercise plan when the user reports chest pain, fainting, severe dizziness, severe shortness of breath, unexplained rapid heartbeat, suspected fracture, severe injury, uncontrolled blood pressure, active eating disorder symptoms, pregnancy complications, kidney disease diet restrictions, or blood glucose emergencies.

For these situations, provide supportive general guidance, recommend medical care, and avoid calorie targets, aggressive weight-loss targets, supplement protocols, or intense exercise prescriptions.
